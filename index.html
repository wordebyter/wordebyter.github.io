Research Interests

My main research interests lie at the intersection of Machine Learning and Mathematics education. Specifically, I am interested in finding novel ways to apply AI for good within online education platforms. Further, I enjoy employing intelligent statistical models to prove the effectiveness of various interventions with RCTs. Finally, I am interested in fine-tuning LLMs such as Llama 3 in order to provide supports for students.
